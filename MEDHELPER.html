MED HELPER

Your daily health Partner

Features of this App

Medicine Reminders

The patient forgets to take their medicine at the correct time and there is no way to remind them to take the medicine. Users can set medicine reminders so that they no longer miss taking their medicines on time.

Medicine Buying

Users can search and buy medicines online using this app. They can either use popular selling sites like, Netmeds, 1mg, PharmEasy, or can choose our local medicine buying page named Medseller. 

Medicine Selling

The local seller sells their product or medicine only through man-to-man, the seller can’t accommodate high level websites and medium for it and face the issues of high commission etc. The local sellers can sell their products through the app and thereby increase their sales.

Online Doctor Consultency

Users can search for doctors, and can take appoinments to consult doctor. 
User also gets online prescriptions with digital signature of doctor, in the form of pdf. 

Logins

Seller

Sellers can create an account using google account. they can update their products, view them, delete them, and can also add updates while delivering products so that customers can track the products.

User

User needs to signup with an email account inorder to use this app. An account is required to backup reminders and buy products. To consult a doctor u will have to create a public profile.

Doctor

Doctor needs to login with their google account, after that they need to verify their account using thir register number. A public profile is required to consult patients online.

About This App

The Medhelper is an all in one medical assistant that can helpful for doctors, patients, sellers.
